Be My Eyes is a free mobile app designed to assist blind and low-vision individuals by connecting them with sighted volunteers and AI-powered services for real-time visual support.

App Features:

Live Video Assistance: Connects users with volunteers via live video calls for tasks like reading labels or navigating environments.

Be My AI: An AI-powered assistant that provides image descriptions and answers questions about visual content.

Business Solutions: Offers services for companies to assist customers with visual impairments through the platform.

Accessibility: Supports over 180 languages and is available in more than 150 countries.
